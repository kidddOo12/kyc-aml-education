Month-by-Month Plan

Your 12-Month Action Roadmap

A practical, week-by-week-style plan to take you from student to hired compliance professional.

Month 1–2
Build Your Foundation
  • Understand KYC/AML regulatory landscape (FATF, BSA, EU AMLD)
  • Learn CDD, EDD, and UBO concepts
  • Study PEP classifications and sanctions lists
  • Set up LinkedIn with compliance keywords
  • Join compliance communities on LinkedIn/Reddit
✦ Outcome: Solid conceptual base, professional profile live
Month 3–4
Get Certified
  • Enroll in ICA Certificate in KYC or ACAMS Foundation
  • Complete all modules + practice exams
  • Study transaction monitoring typologies
  • Practice SAR writing using templates
  • Start CAMS exam preparation material
✦ Outcome: First certification earned, CAMS study started
Month 5–6
Hands-On Practice
  • Work through simulated KYC case studies
  • Practice with Vikas's compliance lab exercises
  • Learn WorldCheck / Refinitiv navigation basics
  • Write 3 sample SARs for your portfolio
  • Complete a mock onboarding workflow end-to-end
✦ Outcome: Practical portfolio of compliance work samples
Month 7–8
Resume & Applications
  • Build ATS-optimized compliance resume with Vikas's template
  • Craft a compliance-specific cover letter
  • Apply to 10–15 entry-level KYC/AML roles per week
  • Target fintech startups, banks, and consulting firms
  • Reach out to 5 compliance professionals per week on LinkedIn
✦ Outcome: Active applications, growing network
Month 9–10
Interview Preparation
  • Practice 30+ common KYC/AML interview questions
  • Prepare STAR-format answers for behavioral questions
  • Run mock interviews with Vikas or peers
  • Research target employers' compliance programs
  • Prepare questions to ask interviewers
✦ Outcome: Confident, interview-ready candidate
Month 11–12
Land the Offer
  • Continue applications and follow-ups systematically
  • Negotiate your offer with market data
  • Accept role and prepare for onboarding
  • Begin CAMS study in parallel for career advancement
  • Celebrate and share your journey #AMLwithVikas
✦ Outcome: Offer accepted, KYC/AML career officially launched 🎉
