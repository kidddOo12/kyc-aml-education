FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

I'm not a commerce graduate. Can I get into KYC/AML? ↓
Absolutely. KYC/AML values analytical thinking and attention to detail over your specific degree. Many successful professionals come from law, arts, or even engineering. Vikas himself has mentored students from every background into successful compliance careers.
How long does it take to get the first job? ↓
With focused learning (3–4 months for certification + 1–2 months for applications), most candidates secure an entry-level role within 6–8 months. Vikas's mentorship helps you skip common mistakes that slow people down.
Do I need coding or complex software skills? ↓
No coding required. You'll use basic office tools and specialized compliance software like WorldCheck and case management systems — all of which are taught on the job or in Vikas's program.
Which certification is best for beginners? ↓
Start with the ICA Certificate in KYC or the ACAMS Foundations Certificate. Then work towards CAMS — the global gold standard that opens doors at top banks and fintechs worldwide.
Is remote work common in compliance? ↓
Yes! A large portion of KYC (document verification, data entry, screening) can be done remotely, and many companies now offer hybrid or fully remote options — especially fintechs and BPO firms.
