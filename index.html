Frequently asked questions

I’m not a commerce graduate. Can I still get into KYC/AML?
Absolutely. KYC/AML values analytical thinking and attention to detail over your specific degree. Many successful professionals come from law, arts, or even engineering.
How long does it take to get the first job?
With focused learning (3‑4 months for certification + 1‑2 months for applications), most candidates secure an entry‑level role within 6‑8 months.
Do I need to know coding or complex software?
No coding required. You’ll use basic office tools and specialized compliance software that’s taught on the job.
Which certification is best for beginners?
CAMS (Certified Anti‑Money Laundering Specialist) is the global gold standard. Many start with入门‑level certificates from ICA or ACAMS.
Is remote work common in compliance?
Yes! A large part of KYC (document verification, data entry) can be done remotely, and many companies offer hybrid/remote options.
What’s the average starting salary?
It varies by region, but entry‑level KYC analysts earn between $45k‑$65k in the US/UK, and competitive packages in Asia and ME.
